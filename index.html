    Help

    1. What is Tastea ?
    Tastea is a delightful dining destination that offers a diverse range of exquisite culinary creations. From delectable appetizers to mouthwatering entrees and decadent desserts, we aim to provide an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.

    2. can I make a reservation ?
    Making a reservation is simple and convenient. You can either give us a call at 90261 37777 or use our online reservation system on our website. Just select your preferred date, time, and the number of guests, and you're all set to enjoy a wonderful meal with us.

    3. What cuisines do you serve ?
    At Tastea, we take pride in our diverse menu inspired by flavors from around the world. Our chefs are skilled in creating dishes that span various cuisines, from traditional to contemporary. You can expect a fusion of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

    4. Can I host private events or parties at your restaurant ?
    Absolutely! We offer event hosting services for private parties, corporate gatherings, and special occasions. Our team will work closely with you to customize the menu and ambiance to suit your event's requirements. Please contact our events team at tastea.events@gmail.com for more information.

    5. What safety measures do you have in place ?
    The safety and well-being of our guests and staff are of paramount importance. We adhere to stringent hygiene and sanitation standards. In light of the ongoing global situation, we have implemented additional safety measures, including spaced-out seating, regular disinfection, and the option for contactless menu access.

    6. How can I provide feedback about my experience ?
    We welcome your feedback as it helps us improve and serve you better. You can share your experience by leaving a review on our website or on popular review platforms. If you wish to provide direct feedback, feel free to speak with the restaurant manager during your visit or email us at tastearestr@gmail.com.

    7. What are your operating hours ?
    Our regular operating hours are 9.00 am - 6.00 pm. We are open monday to saturday for lunch and dinner service. Please note that these hours are subject to change during holidays or special occasions. Check our website or contact us for any updates on operating hours.

    8. How can I join your team ?
    We're always on the lookout for passionate individuals to join our team. If you're interested in career opportunities at Tastea, please visit our Careers page on the website or send your resume to tastearestr@gmail.com.

    For any further assistance or inquiries, don't hesitate to reach out to us at 90261 37777 or tastearestr@gmail.com. We look forward to serving you and providing an exceptional dining experience at tastea.
